The display shows you information regarding the jingles, functionality and time.

The remaining time is displayed only for the last jingle played. It shows seconds and milliseconds in two digits each, and minutes in one digit. If the remaining time is below 1 minute, the the minute digit displays "-" as a sign for remaining time.

The red frame around the remaining time appears to warn you when there are only 3 seconds left of the jingle. The time when it appears and its color can be customized from the settings window.

The clock (½) is displayed only when Time Announce function is turned on. It disappears automatically when announcing finishes.

The red warning sign appears when Jingle Palette has a message for you, in the above example the framed "x" (x) means that there is an error. The red hourglass (6) means that you must wait for something to finish (for example palette loading) and the floppy disk (<) shows that save completed successfully. Moving the mouse above the sign will pop up a balloon with the description of the event. Clicking it will turn the warning off.

The name of the currently selected palette is shown in green, above is the previous palette, below is the next palette.

The clock displays the current time in 24 hour format, or 12 hour AM/PM format. This can be chosen in the Settings window.

The week number is in the middle, it counts the weeks considering that the first day of the week is Monday.

The current date can also be displayed in several ways, see the Settings window for details.

The current day is at the right, right below the clock. Please note that the language of the date and day depends on the regional settings of your computer. Please set correctly the regional settings in Control Panel according to your location.
