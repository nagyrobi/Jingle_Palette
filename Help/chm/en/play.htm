Simple Playback

To Play a jingle, simply click the button with its name:

You can also start playback by pressing the SPACE bar on the selected jingle button, or right-clicking and selecting Play. A button is selected when a black rectangle is present on it (just like in the picture).

While playing, it will turn green and stay pressed so you know that it's ON AIR. The display will show the remaining playback time of the jingle, and will warn you when it reaches its end. Also the VU meter will display the level of the sound.

The green button jumps back and gets back its original color when the jingle reaches to the end and stops.

You can stop the jingle in several ways. Clicking it, pressing SPACE bar on it again or right-clicking and selecting and selecting Stop while it's playing (green state) will stop the playback of the jingle. Pressing STOP ALL will do the same.

You can play several jingles at once. Just click on them in the order you wish, and they will start playing right away. There's no limit in how many jingles play at once. You can even play jingles from different palettes in the same time.

You can stop them one by one by clicking them again while they are playing, or stop all at once by pressing STOP ALL.

Note: This button stops all the audio output from Jingle Palette. This means that if you have Time Announce or a remote stream playing, they will be stopped also. Pressing it also reloads the currently selected palette. Pressing ESCAPE button has the same effect as STOP ALL. You can move around with the selection of buttons (the black rectangle) by pressing TAB or the arrow keys.

If you change the palette while jingles are playing, the state of the corresponding buttons will be raised even if you come back to the palette from which the jingle was launched from. The only way to stop the playing jingles in this case is pressing STOP ALL.

Autorepeated Playback

Autorepeat mode can be turned on by clicking the Autorepeat button, just below the display:

This mode differs from the simple mode in the way that when clicking again the playing jingle (while it's green), instead of stopping, it will play again from the beginning. This is useful for radio DJs when they wish to play ON AIR with some jingles on the beat of some song.

In this mode the only way to stop the jingle is pressing STOP ALL. If you right-click on a jingle while this is on, you will see the option to autorepreat it.

Touch Play

If this is pressed, the jingles will be play only while they are "touched" by mouse, screen or the SPACE key from keyboard.

Autorepeat and Touch Play cannot work together. They cancel each other.

Auto Mix function

This function is useful if you want to fade a playing jingle automatically while another one starts playing.

The mix time is set by default to 1.5 seconds, but this value can be changed in the settings to any value.

Pausing playback of a jingle

Jingles can be paused individually by right-clicking on them while they are playing. Selecting Pause from the pop-up menu will pause playback and will flash the jingle's button. You can resume playback either by clicking the jingle normally again, or by right-clicking and selecting Resume.

You can also stop the paused jingle by right-clicking and selecting Stop.
